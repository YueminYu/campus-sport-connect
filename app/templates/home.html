Campus Sports Connect

Connecting University of Illinois students for sports activities and team events.

Campus Sports Connect is a web application that allows students to organize and join sports events on campus. Whether you are looking to gather players for a basketball game, or you want to join a soccer match, our app makes it easy to connect with fellow students who share your interests and schedules.

Project Prospect

Our platform fosters a vibrant sports community at UIUC by offering an intuitive platform for event creation, discovery, and participation. Through Campus Sports Connect, students can stay active, meet new friends, and enjoy organized sports events seamlessly.
